Salmon and asparagus

Description

This is a recipe for salmon, green asparagus and roasted potatoes.

Ingredients:

  • Salmon
  • Green asparagus
  • Small potatoes
  • Lemon
  • Dill
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Recipe:

  1. Marinade the salmon with olive oil, lemon juice, dill, salt & pepper. Put in oven at 180 degrees for 12 minutes.
  2. Roast the potatoes and grees aparagus in a pan. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Present on a plate with fresh lemon slices.
