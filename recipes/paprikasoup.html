Paprika soup with mascarpone and oregano

Description

This is a recipe for a smoked paprika soup with mascarpone and oregano.

Ingredients:

  • Red paprika
  • Roasted paprika in a jar
  • Onion
  • Paprika powder
  • Vegetable stock
  • Mascarpone
  • Oregano
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Recipe:

  1. Soften onion in the pan.
  2. Add paprika powder and heat until aromatic.
  3. Add fresh paprika, roasted paprika and the vegetable stock.
  4. Cook for 20 minutes until vegetables are soft.
  5. Blend the soup with the mascarpone
  6. Present the soup with some fresh oregano and olive oil.
